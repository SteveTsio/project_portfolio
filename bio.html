My name is Tsiogas Stavros and from a very young age I showed passion for science and inception. I discovered that all scientists had a love for arts and crafts and as I grew up I found out why. I first took up computer sciences and telecommunications and then I turned to graphic and web design. Every day I add new skills into my toolkit as knowledge can never be enough.

Everything started in June of 2015 with my internship in the Greek Film Center where I created from scratch an IT department and I took over the design and implementation of the new website. The level of satisfaction was apparent as the general manager proceeded to my recruitment and assigned me the design of two publications, one for the international festival in Thessaloniki and one for Berlin. Those publications were handed out in both festivals respectively.

In May 2016 I took the position of technical support, representing one of the greatest mobile gaming companies. This helped me cultivate my interpersonal relationships, my soft skills, as well as my ability to form and manage teams. What I also discovered was my ability to train new employees.

In May 2029 I assumed the position of Digital Marketing Manager to a group of companies specializing in providing counseling and training on selection and admission to universities abroad. I was in charge of both the organization and planning of the department as well as the execution of the respective campaign and the analysis of its results.

From February 2020 up until today I have assumed the position of performance digital marketing manager in a marketing agency and my role is the implementation of integrated campaigns for multinational companies mainly in the field of information technology, retail and services.
